SPECIAL SYMBOLS

In the language version given here, the lexems are the following symbols and their combinations:

Combination Meaning
: Colon
[, ] Square brackets
(, ) Round brackets
, Comma
+ Addition sign
- Subtraction sign
* Multiplication sign
/ Division sign
< Less sign
> More sign
= Equality sign
<> Non-equality sign
<= Not more sign
>= Not less sign
.. Range sign
