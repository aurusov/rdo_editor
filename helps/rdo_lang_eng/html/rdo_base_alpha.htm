ALPHABET

The language's alphabet contains the following symbols:

  • digits from 0 to 9;
  • capital and small English letters, A to Z and a to z;
  • capital and small Russian letters, À to ß and à to ÿ;
  • special symbols;
    • _ - underline symbol;
    $ - dollar sign;
    : - colon;
    [, ] - square brackets;
    (, ) - round brackets;
    {, } - figure brackets;
    , - comma;
    + - addition sign;
    - - subtraction sign;
    * - multiplication sign;
    / - division sign;
    < - less sign;
    > - more sign;
    = - equality sign;
    . - full stop sign;
    ' - quotation mark;
    \ - backslash;
    ~ - tilde;
    # - number sign.
