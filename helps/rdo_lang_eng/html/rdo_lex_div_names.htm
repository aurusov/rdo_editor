NAMES

A simple name is a sequence of capital or small letters, Russian or Latin, or digits, or symbols "$" or "_".

A complex name contains two simple names divided by full stop sign.

Remarks

A name cannot start with a digit.

In the language version given here, the length of a simple or a complex name cannot exceed 63 symbols.

Capital and small letters are distinguished, the version is case-sensitive.

Examples

Speed
$Value
Aux_var
_time
Period_between_tankers_arrivals
MachineTool.mode
Storage.volume
Figaro.Queue_length
